_________________________BIRDS___________________________________

Humming Birds are the smallest mature birds, measuring 7.5–13 cm (3–5 in) in length. The smallest is the 5 cm (2.0 in) bee hummingbird, which weighs less than 2.0 g (0.07 oz), and the largest is the 23 cm (9.1 in) giant hummingbird, weighing 18–24 grams (0.63–0.85 oz). Noted for long beaks, hummingbirds are specialized for feeding on flower nectar, but all species also consume small insects.

Birds are beautiful because.....

  1. Hummingbirds have unique, impressive flight abilities, such as being able to fly backwards and upside down.
  2. These small birds have big appetites due to a fast metabolism, and can consume up to 12 times their bodyweight in nectar every day.
  3. They are often highly territorial and fiercely guard their own patch of flowers.

Humming Birds Specilitie.....

  • A hummingbird is a small bird with a long, slender bill. Many hummingbirds have brightly colored, glittery feathers.
  • Often the males are more colorful than the females.
  • The bee hummingbird lives up to seven years in the wild, and 10 years in captivity.
